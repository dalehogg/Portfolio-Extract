485 Design is a multi-awarded company with a talented team of designers and creative thinkers. Our vision is to make your business more remarkable and disruptive.

We can't stand design becoming an aesthetic attachment to business at the end of the process, because we see design as an integrated, strategic, and vital component to propel businesses to be more relevant and drive more value. We are small enough to be agile and responsive - fully involved in your business, while connected into a large network - enabling us to produce anything you need, to deliver an amazing experience. We have a vast amount of experience and capabilities to enhance your brand. Because of this we are more responsive, focused and (if needs require) have a fast turn around.
